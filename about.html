Our Story

Like any other girl, I've always dreamed of having healthy, beautiful hair. My passion for hair and skincare began as a personal journey, full of experiments to find the right solution, especially after the economic crisis and the import halt.

But I always felt something was missing. Imported products were overpriced, while local options often lacked quality or contained harmful chemicals. As a pharmacist, I could read the ingredients and knew which chemicals promised temporary beauty but caused long-term damage.

During the economic downturn, my frustration peaked. Trusted products disappeared or became too expensive. That's when I decided I had to find a solution—not just for me, but for every girl facing the same struggle.

I went back to studying from scratch, like I did in college, embarking on a long journey of research and experiments to develop a truly natural formula.

The journey wasn’t easy. I was torn between fear of failure and excitement about solving many problems. But I was certain the product would be 100% natural and harmless. I asked testers to give honest feedback, highlighting flaws before benefits, so I could improve.

The biggest challenge was convincing people that natural products don't need to "smell fancy" or look glossy. Ingredients like horsetail and lanolin have strong scents, but their benefits are worth it.

The most rewarding moment came when people began requesting the product before I even had labels or a sales page! Messages asking for large bottles in the morning showed me that my passion was worth it.

VeraCare is more than products—it’s the result of a journey of research, passion, and persistence. We now have a dedicated team continuously developing and improving formulas for the best results. We believe in your natural beauty and that every girl deserves confidence without hiding flaws. Our products help girls embrace their natural, healthy hair, free from excessive heat styling.

VeraCare is my promise of honesty and transparency. Every product carries the trust and safety you dream of, and I’ll be with you every step of the way toward your most beautiful, natural self.
